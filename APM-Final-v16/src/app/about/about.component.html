About Acme Product Management

Welcome to Our Product Management System

Acme Product Management is a comprehensive solution for managing your product catalog, built with the latest Angular technologies and best practices.

Key Features
  • Product Catalog: Browse and manage your complete product inventory
  • Advanced Filtering: Find products quickly with real-time search
  • Star Ratings: View customer ratings and feedback
  • Detailed Views: Access comprehensive product information
  • Responsive Design: Works seamlessly on all devices
Technology Stack
  • Framework: Angular {{angularVersion}}
  • Architecture: Standalone Components
  • State Management: Angular Signals
  • Styling: Bootstrap 5
  • HTTP Client: Angular HttpClient
  • Routing: Angular Router
  • Testing: Jasmine & Karma
  • Build Tool: Angular CLI
Built for Teams

Designed with modern development practices including lazy loading, performance optimization, and maintainable code architecture.

Mobile Ready

Fully responsive design ensures your product management system works perfectly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
